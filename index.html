    A Note on Usage

    All functionality should move through ETPush and it’s methods. ETPush is a singleton for your convenience, and will handle communication with ExactTarget automatically. There are a few internal use methods exposed, but should not be used and are documented as such.

    More information is available at Code@ExactTarget.
